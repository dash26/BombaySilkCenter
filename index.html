About

Bombay Silk Centre (BSC), established in 1988, is an exclusive showroom for all silk fabrics and is located in Al Khaleej St, Doha at the heart of Doha. The pioneer at the helm of Bombay Silk Centre is Bekal Mohammed Shali, also known as the “Silkman of Doha”.
